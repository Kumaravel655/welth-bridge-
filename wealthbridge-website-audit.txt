Sub-line: "The default choice for new businesses and developing organizations
as only private limited companies can raise funding."
Timeline: 10 to 15 working days

Description: "A perfect start for anyone who wants to start their business
to give it a separate legal identity." Described as "a type of privately
held small business entity" — limits owner liability to shares, restricts
shareholders to 50 maximum, prevents public share trading.

Key features:
  - Members: minimum 2, maximum 200
  - Prospectus not required
  - Cannot accept public deposits
  - Can raise funds from VCs/angel investors
  - Limited liability for shareholders
  - Minimum 2 directors (no independent directors needed)
  - Minimum capital: ₹1,00,000 (one lakh rupees)

Process steps (likely shown as a 4-step visual timeline):
  1. Applying DIN & DSC
  2. Name Approval
  3. MOA, AOA & Affidavit
  4. Company Registered

Package includes:
  - Verification of documents
  - Professional fee for incorporation
  - Applying DSC, DIN, ROC & TAN
  - Follow-up until registration certificate is allotted

Documents required:
  - PAN card and residence proof of directors
  - Rental agreement / EB card copy for registered office
  - Latest bank statement or utility bills
  - Passport-size photographs
  - Specimen signatures
  - Foreign director's passport copy (if applicable)

passed on 29th March 2017 in the Parliament under the Goods and Service Tax
Act." Effective from July 1, 2017. Unified tax with three components: CGST,
SGST, IGST.
Timeline: 5 to 7 working days
Registration threshold: turnover > ₹20 Lakhs (₹10 Lakhs in special category
states); ₹40 Lakhs for service income.

Documents required:
  - PAN card of the business (companies)
  - PAN and residence proof of partners/directors
  - Business constitution proof
  - Business address proof (owned or rented)
  - Bank statement or cancelled cheque
  - DSC for companies

Package includes:
  - Document verification
  - Filing the online/offline GST registration application
  - Authority liaison until certificate is granted

Process steps:
  1. GST Application Preparation — expert-guided document collection
  2. GST Application Filing — online submission, instant ARN number
  3. GST Registration Certificate — officer verification, GSTIN/certificate
     issuance

NOTE: All ~60 service pages on the current site follow this same repeating
template (headline, timeline badge, description, feature list, numbered
process steps, package inclusions, documents checklist). In the redesign this
should become ONE reusable dynamic page template/component driven by a
content array, not 60 hand-built PHP files.

13. OBSERVED UX/ANIMATION PATTERNS ON CURRENT SITE (for redesign baseline)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Typed.js rotating text in hero headline
- Particles.js animated dot/line background behind hero
- Parallax scrolling section(s) via dzsparallaxer
- Slick carousel for testimonials/services slider
- Fancybox lightbox for image popups
- Mega-menu dropdown navigation with categorized columns
- animate.css scroll-triggered entrance animations
- Hamburger icon animation for mobile nav toggle

These are dated jQuery-era techniques. A modern rebuild should replace them
with: CSS scroll-driven animations / Intersection Observer reveals, Framer
Motion or GSAP for hero + micro-interactions, a lightweight mega-menu built
in the chosen framework, native smooth-scroll, and skeleton/optimistic
loading states — while keeping the same functional patterns (rotating hero
text, animated stats, step timelines, pricing cards, mega nav).
